  • Baked Beans Salad

    1. Put a tin of baked beans in a bowl
    2. Add mayonnaise to the beans and mix it up
    3. Add small chunks/slices of cucumber, tomatoes and carrots
    4. Add salt to taste (optional) 5. Chill for a few minutes or start eating right away.

  • Boiled Egg Salad

    1.Boil 3 eggs. You could more eggs if you need to
    2.After the eggs are done, place them in cold water for few minutes
    3.Peel the eggs ad chop them into small pieces or a size you desire
    4.Place chopped eggs in a bowl and mix with with your choice of mayonaisse
    5.Add chopped red onions, salt and pepper to taste (Optional). 6.Salad can be kept in fridge for up to 3 days. Enjoy!
