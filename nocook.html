• Tuna Sandwich

    Spread your choice of tuna on slice of bread

    Add tomato slices, mayonnaise

    Additional you could add lettuce to complete your sandwich

  • Polony/Ham Sandwich

    YES a Freaking SANDWICH!!

    Get two slices of bread, a croissant or a chunk of bread that has been cut down the middle

    Cut two slices of polony in half and place on the slice of bread to cover the whole area

    Additions: Choose any or all (tomatoes, chesse, cucumbers, cabbage, avacado)

    There you go, you just made a sandwich to fuel yourself

  • Baked Beans Salad

    Put a tin of baked beans in a bowl

    Add mayonnaise to the beans and mix it up

    Add small chunks/slices of cucumber, tomatoes and carrots

    Add salt to taste (optional)

    Chill for a few minutes or start eating right away

  • Boiled Egg Salad

    Boil 3 eggs. You could use more eggs if you need to

    After the eggs are done, place them in cold water for few minutes

    Peel the eggs ad chop them into small pieces or a size you desire

    Place chopped eggs in a bowl and mix with with your choice of mayonnaise

    Add chopped red onions, salt and pepper to taste (Optional)

    Salad can be kept in fridge for up to 3 days. Enjoy!
