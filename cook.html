Peel 3 medium sized potatoes and cut into small pieces/chunks

Add the potatoes, onions and red pepper to a pan on medium heat.

Cook for 7 to 8 mins until potatoes are tender and golden brown

Beat about 3 eggs and add salt to taste in a bowl

Add the beaten eggs to the pan, cook for 3 mins stirring occasionally

Get ready with the following (2 eggs, 1 tablespoon sugar, atleast 2 slices of bread and a dash of cinnamon)

In a small bowl, beat the eggs together with the sugar then soak the bread in the egg mixture

Place the slices of bread in a frying pan on medium heat and cook until done

Sprinkle your french toasst with cinnamon before serving
